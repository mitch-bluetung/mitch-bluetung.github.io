Glen Rey. is the moniker of Mitchell J.G. Reynolds: a musician and electrical engineer originally from regional NSW, currently based in Melbourne/Naarm, Australia.

Refined over multiple full-length releases under the previous name Bluetung, his sonic world-building is steeped in experimental music history with whispers of kosmische, tape music, avant-folk and microsound echoing into a formless ambient space of his own devising.

Reynolds has been releasing works since 2018 on beloved cult labels including Theory Therapy, Altered States Tapes and .jpeg Artefacts, with his newest LP released by Brierfield Flood Press.

He has collaborated and performed with artists such as Mara Schwerdtfeger, Other Joe, Nico Niquo, Troth, Florian TM Zeisig, and Mike Midnight.
