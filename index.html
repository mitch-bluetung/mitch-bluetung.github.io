Glen Rey. is the moniker of Mitchell J.G. Reynolds: a musician and electrical engineer originally from regional NSW, currently based in Melbourne/Naarm, Australia.

Refined over multiple full-length releases under the previous name Bluetung, his sonic world-building is steeped in experimental music history with whispers of kosmische, tape music, avant-folk and minimalist composition echoing into a formless ambient space of his own devising.

Reynolds has been releasing works since 2018 on beloved cult labels including Theory Therapy, Altered States Tapes and .jpeg Artefacts with a new LP prepared for release in early 2025.

He has collaborated and performed with artists such as Mara Schwerdtfeger, Other Joe, Nico Niquo, Troth, Florian TM Zeisig, and Mike Midnight.
